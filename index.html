Coronavirus / covid19 - Do I need to call care?

Do a self-assessment to see if you can self-care or need to contact the care provider.

Most people who are infected with coronaviruses have mild symptoms and can stay home and treat them as if they have a cold or flu.

In an emergency life-threatening condition, do not do this self-assessment without ringing 0800100066.

The purpose of self-assessment is to provide answers if you need care or if you can take care of yourself at home. At the end of the test, you will receive advice tailored to your answers.

Do not use this self-assessment when it comes to children. If you have children that you suspect have been infected, call 0800100066 for guidance.

Self-assessment does not lead to direct contact with health care providers.

No answers are saved to the service, but deleted when you close the page.
